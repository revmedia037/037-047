Discover Your Life Purpose

Uncovering your life purpose brings clarity and direction to your daily actions. Reflect on activities that make you lose track of time and bring you joy. Consider what problems in the world you feel most passionate about solving. Examine past experiences for patterns of what energizes versus drains you. Talk to close friends about when they've seen you at your best. Experiment with different volunteer opportunities to test what resonates. Journal regularly about your values, dreams, and ideal legacy. Remember that purpose often evolves throughout life's different stages. The journey of discovery itself is valuable personal growth.
